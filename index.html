About Bordoichila

New Building

The new building which was recently inaugurated in the year 2020,provides modern facilities and infrastructure to its boarders. The rooms are well equipped and contains ample amount of space for daily activities.

Old Building

The old building established in the year 1999 holds within itself the memories of past 22 years.It has witnessed thousands of boarders growing and blooming in its heart and rising to individuals. 

Hostel Entrance

The new building greets you with a lovely infrastructure, a bright sunbeam and a welcoming entrance.

Security Post

The Security Maahis' are always at your service to protect you and rescue you from any difficult situation while your stay in the hostel.

Visitor's room

Spend quality time with your family while they visit you. The visitor's room provides provision for you and your family to have that personal space and family time when you encounter them after months.

Open Area

While on a balmy day, never miss to sit under the ray!! Bordoichila provides an open space for its borders to dry up clothes and chit-chat with friends enjoying the beautiful sunlight of the winter. 

Sitting Area

The sitting area is for visitors and guests.

Basin Area

The basin area is well equipped with modern architecture and facilities for drinking water.

Room (OB)
